Nice to have

A little bit of The Planning Game, brought to Trello

What is this?

This app brings some of the The Planning Game to Trello. If you have a big backlog (don't we all?) then I recommend giving The Planning Game, and this app, a try.

What this is not

  • This is not a replacement for Trello. It would be super-fun to re-implement Trello, but not a good use of my time. Instead this app tries to do the minimum it needs to do itself, and leaves the rest to Trello.

    This means that whenever you don't see a feature, it's likely Trello already does it.

  • This is not planning poker, or any other estimation method. It's up to you to decide how to assign risk or estimates to tasks/features/whatever. This is just a way to visualise what you've decided.

History

I started using Trello a lot at work and at home. Backlogs were created, and started to grow. I'd seen The Planning Game work well in the past, so I suggested to a colleague that we try it out. I was irked that we needed to write out the cards so that we could play it. I'm open to physical representations, but given we have the backlog in Trello, why can't we do it all online? So, "Nice to have" was born.
